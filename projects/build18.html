Build18

  • I have been a leader of a new club at Carnegie Mellon called Build18. It is a week-long “rapid prototyping” session sponsored by the Electrical and Computer Engineering department (hence the “18”) and many generous companies that provide money for parts. We've had a growing number of officers every year as well as a growing number of very ambitious teams that somehow pull off really cool projects!

Things I Did

  • Developed a tutorial series for teaching the basics of microcontrollers, signal and video processing, and brainstorming new project ideas. Check them out here: http://build18.ece.cmu.edu/wiki/index.php/Tutorial_Series
  • Helped out various teams with their projects as an officer (it's sorta like being a TA, except without the boring grading part!)
  • Made some cool projects! Most of them are on the main page of this site.
