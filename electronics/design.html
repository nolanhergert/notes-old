Notes

I like industrial designers, they're a very interesting breed. They think very long and hard through how a product is going to be used and interacted with, and in the end make their digital renderings look awesome regardless of how difficult the product will be to implement. Engineers can learn a lot from them, and they can take a few tips from engineers!
