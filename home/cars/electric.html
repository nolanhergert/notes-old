Expected lifetime of lithium ion batteries

https://electrek.co/2016/11/01/tesla-battery-degradation/ says Tesla got 500K miles before significant degradation.

Basically varies with how often you fast charge, temperature, and surprisingly with depth of discharge.

A misconception (I think) is that battery university's cycle count is always the cumulative full cycle count, when it's likely a count of partial cycles at the given depth of discharge.
