  • You want 3 sets of 12 reps at a given weight. The first for ___, the next for <more exhaustion>, and the third for <lots of exhaustion> (I still don't believe it)

Spark Author (Dr. John Ratey)

YouTube Link! https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Bmc0ERKfjP0

  • Sufficient exercise is like taking a little bit of Ridulin and a bit of Prozac…it gets rid of the heebie-jeebies that you get from sitting down
  • Exercise boosts your ability to learn…keeps the neuroplasticity up by boosting blood flow.
  • Exercise lowers depression. It gets excess energy out (the “heebie jeebies” that you get from sitting in a chair for too long) as well as inhibits receptors in the brain for a while. 63% reduction in suspensions and referrals because of it! Wow…
  • He recommends working out in the morning / afternoon so that you can receive the adjustments while at work. 5/6 days a week for 30 minutes, getting the sweat going.
  • 90-year-olds, once they did exercise, their genes??? dropped to that of a 60-year-old. (40:15) Helps you stay young! Wow…
  • UC Irvine researcher found that continuing to learn, eating less, and exercise prevented cognitive decline especially in older individuals.
  • Exercise seems to do better than more studying to improve math and science scores. Wow…I'd be game for forced recess in high school / college. Especially college.
  • Aerobic exercise > anaerobic exercise, at least for boosting blood flow and the above effects.
