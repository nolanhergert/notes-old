Pitch Detection

  • Tartini Pitch Detection Program (and papers!) Cross-platform pitch detection algorithm, plus his thesis
  • Great StackOverflow post, particularly on cepstral but includes everything.
  • Pitch Detection (Wikipedia)
  • Can do with time-domain (autocorrelation) techniques very accurately but bad with noise, octaves
  • Can also do in frequency domain with FFT, but not as accurate
  • Third method is to combine their strengths together in a dynamic programming problem: http://pods.binghamton.edu/~hhu1/paper/Zahorian2008spectral.pdf
  • Somehow Roger B. Dannenberg did it back in the 70s with a trumpet, so for simple stuff it can't be too bad…
